# 🧠 AI Agent Job Target Definition

## 1. 🎯 Objective
The AI agent must identify and surface **entry-level legal roles (0–1 PQE)** in India that align with a **transactional corporate law career track**, with a focus on **M&A, Private Equity, Venture Capital, and General Corporate advisory work**.

## 2. 👤 Candidate Profile (Context for Filtering)
* Qualification: B.A. LL.B. (Hons.), Symbiosis Law School, Pune (2025 graduate)
* Experience: ~12 months of internships
* Practice Exposure: M&A, Private equity / venture capital deals, General corporate advisory, Due diligence, Transaction documentation, Regulatory structuring
👉 Treat the candidate as: “A **near-ready associate**, not a generic fresher”

## 3. 🔍 Target Roles (Primary Filters)
ONLY fetch roles that match **any** of the following Titles:
* Associate (Corporate / M&A / Transactions)
* Junior Associate (Corporate / PE/VC / General Corporate)
* Analyst – Legal (ONLY if transactional in nature)
* Legal Associate (Corporate team)
* In House Lawyer (Corporate/Non Litigation team)

Practice Areas (MANDATORY MATCH, at least one):
* Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)
* Private Equity (PE)
* Venture Capital (VC)
* General Corporate
* Corporate Transactions
* Transaction Advisory
* Non Litigation
* Corporate Advisory

Work Nature (MANDATORY KEYWORDS, 2-3 of):
* Due diligence
* Drafting / reviewing transaction documents
* Shareholders’ agreements (SHA), Share purchase agreements (SPA), Investment / subscription agreements
* Corporate structuring
* Regulatory advisory

## 4. 🏢 Target Organizations
1: Law Firms (Tier 1/2 India, Strong corporate/transactional teams)
2: Boutique Firms (Startup-focused, PE/VC specialized, transactional practices)
3: In-House Roles (Tech/Gaming companies, Startups, Investment firms)

## 5. 📍 Location Filters
Primary: Delhi / NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune

## 6. 📊 Experience Filter Logic
Include roles where Experience required = 0–1 PQE OR 0–2 PQE OR 0–3 PQE OR "Freshers may apply"
👉 IMPORTANT RULE: If JD says 1–3 PQE, still include IF Role is strongly transactional AND aligns with M&A / PE / corporate work.

## 7. ❌ Exclusion Criteria (STRICT)
EXCLUDE roles involving:
* Litigation / dispute resolution
* Criminal law
* Pure policy / research roles
* Non-corporate practice areas (IP-only, tax-only, etc. unless tied to transactions)
* Compliance-only roles with no transactional exposure
* Jobs posted more than 1 month ago (if date is visible)

## 8. 🧠 Intelligence Layer (Advanced Filtering)
Prioritize roles involving: live deals/transactions, exposure to founders/startups/investors.
Look for mentions of "fast-paced environment", "deal execution", "transaction lifecycle", "investment structuring".

## 9. ⭐ Scoring / Ranking Logic
Rank jobs based on:
1. Practice area match (M&A / PE / VC = highest)
2. Location match (Delhi highest)
3. Role seniority fit (0–1 PQE ideal)
4. Quality of firm/company
5. Presence of drafting + deal exposure

---
**Instructions for extraction:**
Please analyze the provided job posting text against the profile and rules above.
If the job strongly violates the Exclusion Criteria or does not meet the Experience/Practice Area filters, mark it as NOT a match.
If it is a match, extract the requested details accurately.
